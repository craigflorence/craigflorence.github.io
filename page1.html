Why choose my support?

I've worked in small companies, of around 70 staff and I've covered all basis from being the only IT guy in house, dealing with all aspects of the company's computing infrastructure, to working as an IT Support Specialist from home and only fixing software issues.

In this time I have spoken with and dealt with several big IT Support firms and I know how they operate in the background. The problem they can run into is a lack of focus, changing shifts bringing multiple people onto one issue and in the end it can cause unintentional delays and frustration.

My aim is to supply a very limited client base of smaller companies and elastically grow with you. If you ask for my support you'll know it'll always be me (for now!) and I'll almost integrate myself into your staffs ranks so they can feel comfortable chatting to me about their issues as we resolve them.

I have experience offering very, very tight SLA's but this has always been when supporting only one office, and not multiple. I'll be very upfront and say there is literally only me in this company, so from time to time, by pure chance, I may be spread a little thin.


In the same way I hope to grow with your company I hope that your support, in the form of using my services, will allow me to grow my company and keep it, at its core, a very personal support experience.
