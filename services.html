IT Support

The more staff or less time you have then you might find you cannot keep up with proper IT management.
With the IT Support service we can take care of new employee on boarding, password reset requests and general computer troubleshooting like printers, or programs not working.

Microsoft Azure

Azure is Microsoft's vast cloud offering. Our skills in Azure lie with Azure Active Directory, Group management, Resource Group management, Azure ADDS, Virtual machines, Azure Virtual Desktop, Virtual Networks, NSGs and Exchange Online. We can also manage on premises Microsoft Server instances (Domain Controller, Exchange Server).

Networking

From on premises switches and cable management to vLANs, subnetting and MAC port filtering, we can offer remote support or on premises support.

For new starters we can suggest best network design and equipment procurement.

Office Design and startup

A lot of new businesses hit the ground running, and then, as they grow, start realising the restrictions past decisions have imposed on them. We can advise, and implement, on best practices to ensure from 1 to 1000 staff your systems scale with you.

Website Design

We can set up, design, and manage a simple company website. If you require domains, SSL certificates we will handle those, too.

MSP & CSP

Managed Service Provider and Cloud Service Provider tend to go hand in hand. As an MSP theithelp.site would oversee as much of your IT infrastructure as you want. We will keep it up to date and respond to any issues.

ADHOC Support

Billed at fixed rates, my expertise can be purchased for one-off questions, and support sessions if a contract is not feasible for you.
