Services

IT Support

Proper IT management can be hard with many staff or little time.  The IT Support service can help with new employee on boarding, password reset requests and general computer troubleshooting among other tasks.

I can support:
- Microsoft Windows XP - Windows 11(including retail versions)
- Microsoft Server 2012 - 2019
- Microsoft Exchange Server
- Apple MacOS
- Apple iOS, iPadOS, TVOs, WatchOS
- Android mobile OS
- Most VoIP solutions
- CCTV

Microsoft Azure & Microsoft 365

Azure is Microsoft's vast cloud offering. My skills lie with Azure Active Directory, Group management, Resource Group management, Azure ADDS, Virtual machines, Azure Virtual Desktop, Virtual Networks, NSGs and Exchange Online. I can also manage on premises Microsoft Server instances (Domain Controller, Exchange Server).

Microsoft Office 365 and Microsoft 365 offer the world's most robust and secure Data Loss Prevention (DLP) and productivity tools. Integrating Microsoft's Azure suite with the Office 365 portal mean your documents will be secured, but also easily shareable to maximise productivity. You can even provision virtual desktops for your end users for specialised or sensitive workloads.

Networking & Security

From on premises switches and cable management to vLANs, subnetting and MAC port filtering, I can offer remote support or on premises support. I am familiar with Netgear, Ubiquiti, HP and proficient with Meraki equipment, if you want the most premium hardware.

For new starters I can suggest best network design, topology and equipment procurement.

I also have advanced experience with several antivirus offerings like SOPHOS, CiscoAMP, DrayTek and ESET. Additional layers of security can be added like DNS scanning by OpenDNS and advanced spam filters can be put in place to try and protect your inbox.

Office Design, Startup & VoIP

Many new businesses start strong, but then face limitations from their past choices as they grow. I can help you adopt best practices to ensure your systems scale with you, whether you have 1 or 1000 staff.

I can also help with migrating your existing data from DropBox, Google drive, Yahoo Mail, Gmail all to the Microsoft environment.

My familiarity with VoIP solutions means I can even set you up with a call-centre or simple office phones with physical handsets or virtual soft phones.

Lastly, your on premises infrastructure should be protected physically in order to satisfy accreditations like ISO27001, so good access security and CCTV monitoring should be considered.
